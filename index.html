About the Artist

Kyle is a native Texan artist deeply embedded in Dallas's vibrant EDM scene. His artwork reflects the energy and frequency of electronic music, translating sound waves and beat patterns into visual experiences.

Working from his home studio, Kyle creates each piece with the same passion he brings to the dance floor. You'll often find him at local shows and festivals, not just enjoying the music but connecting with the community and sharing his art through vendor booths.

Known for his warm personality and generous spirit, Kyle has become a beloved figure in the Dallas scene, always ready with a smile, a story, and unique trinkets for fellow music lovers. His art captures the essence of "finding the frequency" - that perfect moment when music, art, and community align.

Each piece in this collection represents Kyle's journey through sound, color, and texture, inviting viewers to discover their own frequency within the chaos and beauty of modern life.
