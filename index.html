About the Artist

Kyle is a native Texan artist deeply embedded in Dallas's vibrant EDM scene. His artwork reflects the energy and frequency of electronic music, translating sound waves and beat patterns into visual experiences.

Working from his home studio, Kyle creates each piece with the same passion he brings to the dance floor. Known for his warm personality, you'll often find him at local shows and festivals, connecting with the community through his art.

Each piece captures the essence of "finding the frequency" - that perfect moment when music, art, and community align.
